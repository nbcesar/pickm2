Settings

Each person picks 1 team to win each week during the regular season.

If your team wins, you move on to the next week.

If your team ties, you lose.

But you can't pick the same team twice.

The last person standing wins. If more than one person is alive at the end of the season or if all remaining players lose during the same week, the pot is split between them.

The house take 10% for administrative fees
